In Detail

Data analysis is a novel approach in college education. The primary goal for higher education institutions is to provide quality education for better placement opportunities. By analyzing student performance, this can predict student selection in placements. This algorithm helps identify students who are eligible for different companies. By analyzing student CGPA, skill score, and history of arrears this can find students eligible for various companies. This system can be used as an application for placement offers in the college to manage student information regarding placements.
